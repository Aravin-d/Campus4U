BPT

Bachelor of Physiotherapy (BPT) in India is a four-year undergraduate program that trains students in physiotherapeutic techniques and exercises. Students study anatomy, biomechanics, and clinical subjects, gaining practical skills through internships. BPT graduates play a crucial role in healthcare, assisting patients in recovering from injuries, surgeries, or illnesses. With the increasing emphasis on holistic health, the scope for BPT professionals is expanding. They find opportunities in hospitals, rehabilitation centers, sports clinics, and private practice. BPT offers a rewarding career, contributing to the well-being and mobility of individuals across diverse healthcare settings.
