BSC FASHION DESIGNING

The Bachelor of Science (BSc) in Fashion Designing in India is a dynamic three-year undergraduate degree program that immerses students in the world of fashion. The curriculum covers design principles, textile technology, garment construction, and fashion business concepts. Students develop creative and technical skills, participate in fashion shows, and gain hands-on experience. The BSc Fashion Designing course prepares graduates for careers in the thriving fashion industry, offering opportunities in design houses, apparel manufacturing, and entrepreneurial ventures in India's vibrant fashion landscape.


BSC INTERIOR DESIGNING

The Bachelor of Science (BSc) in Interior Designing in India is a captivating three-year undergraduate program that delves into the art and science of creating functional and aesthetically pleasing interior spaces. Students explore design concepts, space planning, furniture design, and color theory. The curriculum combines theoretical knowledge with practical applications, fostering creativity and technical proficiency. Graduates of the BSc Interior Designing course are equipped for careers in interior design firms, architectural studios, and the dynamic realm of spatial aesthetics in India.


BSC GRAPHIC DESIGNING

The Bachelor of Science (BSc) in Graphic Designing in India is an engaging three-year undergraduate program that focuses on visual communication through digital and print media. Students learn design principles, digital tools, and multimedia techniques. The curriculum integrates creativity with technology, covering areas such as typography, branding, and illustration. Graduates of the BSc Graphic Designing course are prepared for careers in advertising, publishing, and digital media, contributing to India's dynamic and evolving design industry with their innovative visual communication skills.

Students delve into animation techniques, 3D modeling, and game design. The curriculum combines artistic creativity with technical expertise, preparing students for dynamic careers in the animation and gaming industry. Graduates of the BSc Animation and Gaming course contribute to India's thriving entertainment sector, showcasing their skills in animation studios, game development companies, and digital media enterprises.

Students delve into design thinking, prototyping, and materials science. The curriculum blends theoretical knowledge with hands-on experience, fostering a deep understanding of user needs and market trends. Graduates of the B.Des Product Designing course are equipped to pursue careers in industries ranging from consumer electronics to furniture, contributing to India's design landscape with their expertise in creating aesthetically pleasing and user-centric products.
