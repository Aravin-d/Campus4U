ENGINEERING COURSES



FIRE AND SAFETY

Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.) in Fire and Safety Engineering is a four-year undergraduate program in India. Tailored to meet the growing demand for safety professionals, the curriculum encompasses fire science, risk assessment, industrial safety, and emergency response. Students gain theoretical knowledge and practical skills through hands-on training and internships. Graduates are equipped for roles as safety officers, fire safety engineers, and consultants, contributing to safeguarding industries. Institutions like the National Institute of Fire Engineering and Safety Management (NIFE) offer this specialized program to address safety concerns across diverse sectors.


FOOD TECHNOLOGY

Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.) in Food Technology Engineering is a four-year undergraduate program in India. This specialized course integrates principles of engineering and technology with the science of food production and processing. Students learn about food chemistry, microbiology, quality control, and safety standards. The curriculum includes practical training, industrial visits, and projects to equip graduates for roles in the food industry. As demand for processed and safe food rises, this program at institutions such as the Institute of Chemical Technology (ICT) prepares students for careers in food processing, research, and development.


CHEMICAL ENGINEERING

Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.) in Chemical Engineering is a four-year undergraduate program in India.
